01 / identity

What is Kloppy?

Kloppy is a local-first desktop companion concept for people who want their computer to feel a little more alive, a little more useful, and a lot more suspicious of their file names.

It is parody-inspired without borrowing protected characters, names, logos, or assets. The vibe is retro utility software with a tiny chaotic assistant living in the corner.

02 / motive

Why?

Because modern software got very smooth and very boring.

Kloppy is for the part of your brain that misses weird desktop utilities, tiny popups, strange companions, and tools that had enough personality to become workplace folklore.

03 / roadmap

Planned Features

Local-first companion

Keep the small useful bits close to your machine, with the desktop as home base.

Reminders and notes

Capture the thing before it becomes a haunted sticky note in your brain.

Folder watching

Let Kloppy notice downloads, project folders, and suspicious file piles.

Scriptable automations

Run tiny workflows that clean up repeat tasks without getting too ceremonial.

Desktop popups

Receive helpful nudges, questionable encouragement, and dramatic little alerts.

Optional local AI later

Someday, add private machine-local smarts for people who enjoy experiments.
